Simple & Fast

How it works

Using Cigar Hunter takes seconds — no registration required to browse the map.

1

Open the App

Visit cigar-hunter.web.app in your browser. No download, no account needed to start exploring.

2

Find a Shop

The map loads nearby cigar shops automatically. Filter by humidor quality or search any European city.

3

Scan a Cigar

Sign in with Google, tap the Scan button, photograph the band, and let AI do the rest in seconds.

4

Save & Share

Rate it, add a note, save it to your collection, and share it with fellow enthusiasts.

Install in seconds

Cigar Hunter is a Progressive Web App. Add it to your home screen and it works just like a native app — offline support included.

iPhone / iPad
  • 1
    Open cigar-hunter.web.app in Safari
  • 2
    Tap the Share button ⎙ at the bottom of the screen
  • 3
    Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  • 4
    Tap Add — the app icon appears on your home screen
🤖 Android
  • 1
    Open cigar-hunter.web.app in Chrome
  • 2
    Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots) in the top right
  • 3
    Tap "Add to Home screen"
  • 4
    Tap Install — done! Works offline too.
